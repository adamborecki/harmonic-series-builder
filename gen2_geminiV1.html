Why it matters

The harmonic series is the fundamental basis of sound color (timbre) and harmony. Every note you hear on a piano or guitar actually contains a hidden ladder of higher frequencies called "partials."

By learning how to spell these partials, you connect the physics of sound directly to music theory!
